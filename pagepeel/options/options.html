Welcome to PagePeel

A clean text extractor for any webpage. Here's the 30-second tour:

  1. Click the toolbar icon on any page to extract — the popup shows a preview, save buttons, and a summary of what was removed.
  2. Press Alt+Shift+E to save without opening the popup. The icon shows the token estimate after each extraction; amber means there's a warning to check.
  3. Choose your output below — Markdown, JSON, or both. Toggle hyperlinks and images on or off to control file size.
  4. See your history with View extraction history at the bottom — every save is recorded so you can confirm a long run had no missed pages.
