Welcome to CleanLift

A faithful webpage extractor for LLM pipelines. Here's the 30-second tour:

  1. Click the toolbar icon on any page to extract — popup shows a preview, three save buttons, and a stripped-element breakdown.
  2. Press Alt+Shift+E for one-shot save without the popup. The badge shows your token estimate; amber means there's a warning to check.
  3. Tune per-site with the rules below — global custom selectors apply everywhere; per-domain rules only fire on matching hosts.
  4. Audit a batch with View extraction log at the bottom — every attempt is recorded so you can confirm a 200-page run had no silent failures.

Boilerplate filtering
